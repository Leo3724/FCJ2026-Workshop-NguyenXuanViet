## Introduction

After a truly memorable internship journey with FCJ, I feel very grateful and would like to send my sincere thanks to the team. This experience was not only about working, but also a meaningful path full of learning and joyful moments. Below are a few personal reflections and small suggestions, which I hope can be helpful for FCJ.

---

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working environment

Honestly, the working environment at FCJ is what I liked the most. Everyone is friendly and open, and I never felt left behind. Even when I asked “basic” questions, I always received enthusiastic support—sometimes even outside working hours. The workspace is tidy and comfortable as well, which helped me stay focused and get into the rhythm quickly.

### 2. Support from mentors

I’m truly grateful to my mentor for the patience and detailed guidance in each session. What I appreciated most is that my mentor always gave me space to try and solve problems on my own. Instead of providing direct answers, my mentor taught me how to find the answers. That’s one of the most valuable lessons I gained.

The mentor team was also incredibly supportive—quick with procedures and always creating favorable conditions so interns like me could focus on learning and working. With their help and the workshops, I was able to keep pace with the project and support my team in deploying the project successfully.

### 3. Relevance of the work to my academic major

The assigned tasks were closely connected to what I learned at university. More importantly, I had the chance to explore new areas and practical skills that textbooks don’t fully cover. As a result, I strengthened my fundamentals while also leveling up real-world skills—especially around cloud infrastructure, AWS services, and responsible teamwork.

### 4. Learning and skill development opportunities

This internship helped me gain many new skills: using professional project management tools, collaborating effectively in a team, and communicating in a more professional way in a company environment. My mentor’s real-world sharing also helped me shape a clearer career direction.

### 5. Culture and team spirit

FCJ’s culture is great: respectful and professional, but never rigid. When deadlines were tight, everyone worked together and supported one another regardless of role. Feeling like a real part of the team—even as an intern and community member—was genuinely meaningful.

### 6. Internship policies / benefits

I really appreciated the flexible working hours when needed. The opportunity to join workshops (knowledge and skill sharing) was also a big plus, because it showed real investment in interns’ growth and development.

## Additional questions

### What was the most satisfying part of your internship?

What satisfied me the most was the company culture and the strong support from the mentor/team. These are key factors that help FCJ members grow and feel connected.

### What do you think should be improved for future interns?

It would be great to add more workshops so members can better understand the learning roadmap, exchange knowledge, and fill gaps in time to keep up with the project’s progress.

### Would you recommend this internship to a friend? Why?

Definitely YES. This is a place where you can truly challenge yourself and learn from A to Z in a serious way, rather than doing only administrative tasks. The friendly environment and strong support from the team are not easy to find elsewhere.

## Suggestions and expectations

### Any suggestions to improve the internship experience?

I suggest considering a small budget for regular monthly Team Lunch / Team Bonding activities so everyone can relax and connect more.

### Would you like to continue this program in the future?

I would really love the opportunity to continue contributing and growing with FCJ in the future, if there is a suitable position. I also hope I can help support and develop future generations of the community.

### Other comments (free sharing)

I sincerely thank FCJ for creating such a meaningful and valuable internship experience. This was not only a workplace, but also a place where I grew a lot. I hope the program continues to develop and brings great opportunities to more young people.
